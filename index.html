I am a third-year PhD student in the Graphics&Geometric Computing Laboratory (GCL) at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) advised by Prof. Juyong Zhang. My research interests mainly focus on:

  • Neural Representation and Neural Rendering
  • Text to Speech, Voice Cloning

I'm the reviewer of IEEE Transactions on Multimedia (TMM)
